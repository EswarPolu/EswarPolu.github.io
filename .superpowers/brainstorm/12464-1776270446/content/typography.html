A: Outfit + JetBrains Mono

D: Bebas Neue + Instrument Sans

Display: Bebas Neue — tall, condensed, iconic. Think movie posters, SpaceX mission patches. Maximum vertical impact.

Body: Instrument Sans — your current body font, familiar and reliable.

Feel: Monumental + cinematic. The tallest, loudest option. Pure movie-poster energy.

You can also mix — e.g., "D's display font with A's monospace labels." The display font sets the personality; the body font handles readability.
